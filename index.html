I have a master in Mathematics, with specialization in Mathematical Logic and Theoretical Computer Science.

My passion for Front-End development began when I found out that it's a science that requires both my mathematical - engineering approach and my creativity.

A good Front-End developer has to be scientifically prepared, in order to deliver optimal and logical code; a good engineer, to solve complex problems of performance that almost never depend on factors under his control; a code-lover, to write reusable, clean and scalable code; he has to be creative, with a taste for style and web design, but in the same time he has to care about simmetry and be obsessively precise, otherwise his creativity won't be optimally delivered on a browser; he needs always to put himself in place of his users, and find the solution that optimizes usability, performance and eye pleasingness.

My mission is: give my users the best experience under all the aspects, make them surprised of things that can be done on a browser, using the browser standards that allow my creations to be delivered anywhere in the world.

When I do this I feel gratified, this gratification being the exact reason why I decided to become a software developer.
